Customizable Makeup Packages and Important Details

Looks and Payment Information

Looks can be customized in every package: Matte-finish, Skin-like finish, Glossy-finish, Soft-Glam. Each package is differentiated based on the product’s quality, and the makeup's stay duration also varies by package. Hair extensions like Jadai - ₹300, Curls - ₹500, Straight - ₹500, Kunjalam - ₹200 will cost extra. Jewels & Mehendi are not included in the packages.

A 20% payment is required to block your big day, and no refund will be issued in case of cancellation. The remaining 80% should be paid before the function date. Travel charges may vary based on distance, and accommodations should be arranged for distant clients.

All makeup provided is water-resistant and transfer-proof; however, sweat-proof makeup is a myth!

Essential Tips for Your Big Day

Bridal Preparation Tips for a Flawless Wedding Day Look

Dear brides, it’s highly recommended that you allocate a separate room for your makeup session as it will be incredibly helpful for us. On your special day, please take good care of your makeup to ensure it lasts as long as possible. In preparation for your big day, remember to focus on your health and skincare. Drink plenty of water and, if possible, start a skincare routine to maintain glowing skin. Make it a habit to wash your face regularly for the best results. Please avoid face waxing as it may affect your makeup. Instead, groom your eyebrows and remove any facial hair about three days prior to the event for optimal makeup application. If needed, a touchup will be available for ₹1,700. I also offer a bridesmaid package—feel free to ask for more details.

Our Bridal Makeup Packages

We offer a range of packages, including Silver, Gold, Diamond, and Platinum HD makeup, each tailored to meet your specific needs and budget.

Additional Costs for Hair Extensions

Yes, hair extensions like Jadai, Curls, Straight Hair, and Kunjalam come at an extra cost ranging from ₹200 to ₹500 depending on the type.

Securing Your Booking

To secure your booking, a 20% deposit is required. The remaining balance must be paid before the function date.

Preparation for Your Appointment

Please ensure you have a separate room for makeup, take care of your skin leading up to the big day, and avoid face waxing. Regular washing and hydration are also important.

Duration of Bridal Makeup

The bridal makeup session typically takes around 2-3 hours, depending on the chosen package and additional services like hair extensions.

Makeup Trial

Yes, we offer a half-face trial for free with some packages. A full-face makeup trial can be arranged at an additional cost, allowing you to experience the final look before your big day.

Bridesmaid Package

The bridesmaid package includes basic makeup, hairstyling, and a simple touch-up, providing a cohesive look for your bridesmaids. Please inquire for detailed pricing and customization options.
